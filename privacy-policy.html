Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Lean StartApp ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile applications (collectively, the "Apps").

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including the device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our Apps, such as the features you use, the time spent on the Apps, and other usage statistics.
  • Crash Data: If our Apps crash, we collect data about the crash to help us improve the stability of our Apps.
  • Log Information: We collect log information when you use our Apps, including search terms, app crashes, and system activity.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Apps
  • Develop new features and services
  • Understand how users use our Apps
  • Monitor and analyze trends and usage
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Protect against harmful or illegal activity

Sharing of Information

We may share information as follows:

  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting, analytics, and customer service.
  • Analytics Partners: We use analytics services such as Google Analytics to collect and analyze information about the use of our Apps.
  • Advertising Partners: We may work with third-party advertising companies to show you ads that are targeted to your interests.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Your Choices

You can opt out of certain data collection through your device settings or by uninstalling our Apps. You can also request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address below.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@leanstart.app
